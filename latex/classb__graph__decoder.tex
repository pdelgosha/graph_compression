\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Decodes a simple unmarked bipartite graph. 

Decodes a simple bipartite graph given its encoded integer. We assume that the decoder knows the left and right degree sequences of the encoded graph, hence these sequences must be given when a decoder object is being constructed.
