generates a random graph which is nearly regular, and the degree of each vertex is close to 2 * half_deg. Each vertex is uniformly connected to half_deg many other vertices, and multiple edges are dropped. Furthermore, each vertex and edge is randomly assigned marks, where the vertex mark set is \(\{0,1,\dots, \verb+ver_mark+ - 1\}\) and \(\{0,1,\dots,\verb+edge_mark+ - 1\}\).

Returns
A random graph, where each vertex chooses its degree according to Poisson(deg_mean), then picks neighbors uniformly at random, and connects to them (if the neighbors have not already connected to them, if some of the neighbors I pick are already connected to me, I just don't do anything).
